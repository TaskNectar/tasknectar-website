What Is ROI?

ROI stands for Return on Investment. It's a popular metric used to evaluate how profitable an investment is compared to its cost. Whether you’re spending on marketing, tools, services, or equipment, ROI helps you measure the efficiency of that spend.

The basic ROI formula is: (Return − Investment) ÷ Investment × 100. This gives you a percentage that reflects how much return you made for every unit of currency spent. A positive ROI means you gained value; a negative ROI means you lost money.
